Lemon Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • Softened Unsalted Butter 125g
  • Caster Sugar 125g
  • The zest of 1 Lemon
  • Lemon Juice 2 tbsp
  • 2 Eggs
  • Self-Raising Flour 150g

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  2. Line a normal twelve hole cupcake pan.
  3. In a bowl, beat the butter, sugar and lemon zest until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
  5. Then add in the flour and lemon juice and mix until everything is combined.
  6. Evenly, spoon the mixture into the cupcake cases.
  7. Bake for about 25 minutes or until the toothpick comes out with a few crumbs.

Note:

Make your own self-raising flour by adding 2 tsp of baking powder to 150g of all-purpose flour.
