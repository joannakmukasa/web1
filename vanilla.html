Vanilla Cupckaes

Ingredients

  • Self Raising Flour 175g
  • Caster Sugar 175g
  • Butter 175g
  • 3 Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract 1 tsp

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 18 degrees celsius.
  2. Line a 12 hole cupcake pan with cupcake cases.
  3. Using an electric mixer, combine the butter and sugar until the mixture is smooth.
  4. Then add the eggs and vanilla extraxt and mix well.
  5. Lastly, gentrly stor in the self raising flour.
  6. Evenly distribute the mixture between the 12 cupcake cases.
  7. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.

Note:

Add 35g of Sprinkles to make then funfetti cupcakes.
