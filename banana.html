Banana Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour 1 1/2 cups
  • Baking Powder 1 tsp
  • Baking Soda 1 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • 3 Large Bananas
  • Caster Sugar 3/4 cup
  • 1 Egg
  • Melted Butter 1/3 cup

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 170 degrees celsisus.
  2. Line a Twelve hole cupcake pan.
  3. Sift the flower, baking powder, baking soda and salt and mix.
  4. Mash up the bananas.
  5. Mix together the bananas, sugar, egg and melted butter.
  6. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until smooth.
  7. Scoop the mixture into the cupcaks pans.
  8. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
