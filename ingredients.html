Know your Ingredients

Flour, butter, sugar and eggs are the four most important ingredients in baking cupcakes. They are used a majority of the recipes youâ€™ll find anywhere. Below I have outlined what role they play in the whole baking process.

Flour: All-purpose flour is simply plain flour while self-raising flour is plain flour mixed with baking powder and salt. The baking powder in self-raising acts as a raising agent.

Eggs: Eggs are used as a thickener, binding agent, emulsifier and they provide structure to the batter.

Sugar: Sugar adds sweetness of course but it also does much more. It aids in creating softness, locking in moisture and enhancing the other flavours.

Butter: In baking, butter is used in many different forms. These include oil and margarine. They all serve the same purpose. They increase softness in the cupcakes, especially when mixed with sugar first. They also seal in the moisture and increase the shelf life of the baked goods.
