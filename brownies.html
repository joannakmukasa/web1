Brownies

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil 3/4 cup
  • Sugar 1 1/2 cups
  • Vanilla Extract 1 1/2 tsp
  • 3 Eggs
  • All Purpose Flour 3/4
  • Cocoa Poweder 1/3 cup & 2 1/2 tbsp
  • Baking Powder 1/4 tsp & 1/8 tsp
  • Salt 1/4 tsp

Method

  1. First pre heat the oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  2. Then grease a baking pan and line it with baking paper.
  3. Next mix together the oil, sugar and vanilla extract.
  4. To that mixture, add the eggs and mix properly.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, cocoa powder, baking poder and salt.
  6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until it is fully combined.
  7. Pour the batter into the pre greased baking pan.
  8. Lastly, place in the oven and bake for 25-30 mins.

Note:

Feel free to adjust the baking time to your liking. For more gooey brownies, take them out when the toothpick comes out with a small about of batter. For more cake like brownies, wait until the toothpick comes out with crumbs.
