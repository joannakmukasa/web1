My Collection

I have been baking for about 6 years. Over that time, I have collected an array of different tools and utensils that I use to help me. These are all common items that can be found in almost any home. I believe it is important to not mix the tools used in cooking and the tools used in baking to prevent the taste of the baked goods from being affected by strong foods like onions.
