Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

The Dough
  • All Purpose Flour 240g
  • Sugar 25g
  • Baking Powder 1 tbsp
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Cold Butter, cubed 85g
  • Cold Milk 180ml
The Filling
  • Brown Sugar 165g
  • Melted Butter 40g
  • Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
  • Cinnamon Powder 2sp
The Glaze
  • Icing Sugar 160g
  • Hot Water 2 1/2 tbsp
  • Vanilla Extract 1 tsp

Method

The Dough
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  2. Grease and line pan with parchment paper.
  3. Combine the flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
  4. Rub the butter into the flour mixture.
  5. Add the milk and mix until a workable dough is formed.
  6. Shape the dough into a rectangle then roll it out until it is about 25cm by 30cm.
The Filling
  1. Mix together the sugar, melted butter, vanilla extract and cinnamon into a thick paste.
  2. Then carefully spread the filling over the dough.
  1. Slowly and carefully roll the dough into a log lengthwise.
  2. Cut the dough into 9 or more equal pieces and place them into the pan.
  3. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
  4. Allow the cinnamon rolls to cool for about 10 minutes before adding the glaze.
The Glaze
  1. Mix the hot water, vanilla extract and icing sugar until it forms a thick glaze.
  2. Drizzle or pour the glaze over the warm cinnamonrolls and enjoy.

Note: This recipe was gotten from The Gardening Foodie.
